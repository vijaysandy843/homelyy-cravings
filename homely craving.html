Masala Dosa

Crispy dosa filled with spicy potato stuffing. South Indian favorite!

View Recipe
Ingredients:
Dosa batter, potatoes, onion, mustard, curry leaves, turmeric

Steps:
1. Prepare potato stuffing with spices.
2. Spread dosa on hot tawa.
3. Add stuffing and fold.
4. Serve with chutney and sambar.

Gobi Manchurian

Spicy, tangy fried cauliflower bites. Perfect evening snack!

View Recipe
Ingredients:
Cauliflower, corn flour, soy sauce, chili sauce, garlic, spring onions

Steps:
1. Boil and coat gobi with flour mixture.
2. Deep fry till crispy.
3. Toss in Manchurian sauce.
4. Garnish with spring onion and serve.

Veg Biryani

Fragrant rice cooked with mixed vegetables and spices.

View Recipe
Ingredients:
Basmati rice, veggies, curd, biryani masala, mint, saffron

Steps:
1. Marinate vegetables with curd & spices.

3. Layer rice and veggies; cook on dum.

Upma

Quick and healthy South Indian breakfast with semolina.

View Recipe
Ingredients:
Rava, mustard seeds, curry leaves, onion, water, salt

Steps:
1. Roast rava and keep aside.
2. Temper mustard, curry leaves, onion.
3. Add water, boil, then add rava.
4. Stir well and cook until soft.

Mango Lassi

Refreshing chilled drink made with ripe mango and yogurt.

View Recipe
Ingredients:
Ripe mango, yogurt, sugar, cardamom, ice cubes

Steps:
1. Blend mango pulp, yogurt, sugar and cardamom.
2. Add ice and blend again.
3. Pour into glass, garnish with mint.

View Recipe
